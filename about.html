Who We Are

We are Team EC Bin, a group of students focused on using technology to make waste management smarter and cleaner. Our goal is to make disposal easy, rewarding, and eco-friendly.

Our Idea

The Smart Trash Bin Sensor identifies waste types like organic, plastic, and metal using chemical detection. When a user throws waste into the correct bin, the system gives them eco-points which they can use in our connected mobile app and website.

Environmental Impact

Our project supports the 3R principles:

  • Reduce: By helping people throw waste correctly, less trash ends up mixed or wasted.
  • Reuse: Proper sorting means reusable materials are easily collected and repurposed.
  • Recycle: Clear waste separation improves recycling efficiency and lowers pollution.

Our Vision

We aim to sell our smart sensors to bin manufacturing companies. These companies can then install our sensors in their bins and sell them to large communities or cities, helping promote clean and sustainable living.
